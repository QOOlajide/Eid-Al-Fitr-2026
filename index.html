Eid Prayer Guide

Understanding the Eid Prayer

The Eid prayer is a special congregational prayer performed on the morning of Eid al-Fitr. It consists of two rak'ahs with additional takbirs.

1

Intention (Niyyah)

Make the intention to perform the Eid prayer for the sake of Allah

2

First Rak'ah

Begin with 7 additional takbirs, then recite Al-Fatiha and another surah

3

Second Rak'ah

Stand up and perform 5 additional takbirs, then complete the prayer

4

Khutbah

Listen to the khutbah (sermon) delivered by the Imam

Important Notes:

  • Eid prayer is performed in congregation
  • It's recommended to eat something sweet before the prayer
  • Wear your best clothes for the occasion
  • Give Zakat al-Fitr before the prayer

Zakat al-Fitr

What is Zakat al-Fitr?

Zakat al-Fitr is a charitable donation given before the Eid prayer. It purifies those who fast from any indecent act or speech and helps the poor and needy.

Amount

$10 per person (recommended amount)

Timing

Must be given before the Eid prayer

Recipients

Poor and needy in the community

Make Your Payment

Total: $10
